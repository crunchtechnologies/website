My statement

I believe in the future of affordable electronics. With the digital age at it's peak electronics should be more affordable than ever. I seek to be able to provide quality electronics for a low price to people everywhere. Currently based in the states, I can help provide these electronics to anyone in the continental United States. To me, being in the digital age means being connected to anyone/anything, anywhere, anytime with relative ease. Being able to do this you need a variety of items; I can provide these things such as headphones, chargers, and accessories.

A little about me  

In my early years I grew up with an eye for money. My first hustle was during Halloween. I would go around and collect candy from the neighbors then sell them at school the days after. Any and all candy would be gone within a couple days. This is what really started my journey into the money-making empire.

Eventually I decided that making 25 cents on pieces of candy wasn't the best I could do. I taught myself the skills of building computers. I bought, built, and sold computer for a couple of years. Moving forward I learned more skills such as computer repair, programming, and even as far as fixing cellular phones.

I love to be challenged with logical problems. This is why the electronics and programming field is such a passion for me. It is all logically and mathematically based. This gives me an advantage over those who are more creatively minded, but can also set me back as I tend to have a problem coming up with my own activities and projects to work on.
